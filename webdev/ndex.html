Images in html

in html we can add images using img tag

example on adding a img tag : <img src="https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1461749280684-dccba630e2f6?q=80&w=2069&auto=format&fit=crop&ixlib=rb-4.0.3&ixid=M3wxMjA3fDB8MHxwaG90by1wYWdlfHx8fGVufDB8fHx8fA%3D%3D" alt="image1" width="400">

here src means source / desitinatino of that particular image

in this case https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1461749280684-dccba630e2f6?q=80&w=2069&auto=format&fit=crop&ixlib=rb-4.0.3&ixid=M3wxMjA3fDB8MHxwaG90by1wYWdlfHx8fGVufDB8fHx8fA%3D%3D is the source for that particular image

alt helps us to find that particular name in a shorter way , in this case image1 is alternat text

width this helps to add width to a particular image , by adding width img automatically choose its desired height to make the image look in perfect fit

video tags in html

in html we have several video tags , below are some

video tag is used to use video / media queries in html

control provides controls to a video , eg : play , pause , forward, backword etc

loop loop the video , automatically plays after the video ends

muted automatically mute the video , good for better user experience

autoplay autoplays the video after website refreshes

preload preloads the video , we have auto, meta, and none

error is nothing , but if any case video can't able to play , then instead of video this error message will display

below example of a video

<video src="alu.mp4" controls loop autoplay muted width="600" >error</video>
